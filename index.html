ONE

Doublin is a two-player puzzle arcade game about goblins, wizards, and typing.

Made by Brendan Hoag, Dante Barbosa-Toney, Ako Munkhbayar & Mosadi Pearson for GMAP-377 at Drexel University.

TWO

You play as two gobblins in a wizard robe. In order to prevent their cave from being reposessed by the goblin IRS, they enter a wizard tournament disguised as the wizard they killed. There's just one problem: they don't know how to fight like a wizard. Luckily, they also grabbed the wizard's grimoire.
